Our Story

Founded in 2021 in the heart of Bangalore, Grand Foods India emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to create delicious, honest food that brings people together. What started as a dream has evolved into a thriving food manufacturing company that serves thousands of retailers across multiple states.

Our journey began with premium muffins in 2022, and through relentless commitment to quality and innovation, we've expanded our portfolio to 125 SKUs. From our initial 10,000 sq ft facility to our current state-of-the-art 20,000 sq ft manufacturing hub, every step has been guided by our core values of quality, trust, and customer satisfaction.
